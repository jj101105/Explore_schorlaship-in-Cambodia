AUPP University

Originally established in 2013, the American University of Phnom Penh (AUPP) is a premier private university in Cambodia offering American-style education. AUPP provides a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, including Business, Law, Global Affairs, Engineering, and Technology.

Facilities

  • Library
  • Computer Labs
  • Student Housing
  • Audio Hubs
  • Cafeteria
  • Student Lounge

Vision

AUPP aims to be a leading academic center of excellence in Cambodia and Asia.

Mission

AUPP is a private, English-language higher education institution in Cambodia, offering high-quality, American-style education grounded in Cambodian and Asian culture.
