BELTEI International University
(BELTEI)

BELTIE offers various scholarships and financial aid to support both local and international students. Here are the main types of scholarships available:

  • 1. Four-year scholarships for the Head of Royal Government in order to distribute to students who are financially challenged.
  • 2. 50% scholarships for BELTEI's students who completed high school diploma and want to study at BELTEI International University.
  • 3. 50% scholarships for Bachelor's Degree, and 30% for Master's Degree to BELTEI's staff who wish to improve their education skills.
  • 4. Scholarships for BELTEI outstanding students and ones who are finacially challenged.
  • 5. Oknha Ly Chheng 100% scholarships are for BELTEI International University students who wish to continue their study in Bachelor's and Master's Degrees overseas.

BELTIE University

BELTEI International University was established in 2012 under the development of BELTEI International Institute, which was originally established in 2002. BELTEI International University is a private university which locates in Boeung Kengkang 3.

Vision

BELTEI International University will become the most well-known university in Cambodia with high quality of education in alignment with national program and international program standards, which is recognized worldwide and in accordance with the BELTEI's slogan: BELTEI International University The Future of Global Leaders.

Mission

The university provides comprehensive education that develops students' knowledge, critical thinking, communication, and professional skills, while fostering national pride, leadership, ethical behavior, and offering opportunities for higher education from bridging courses to doctoral degrees, with a focus on international alignment and guest speaker presentations to enhance learning.

🌍 Exchange Program

The BELTEI Overseas Student Exchange Program is all about giving students the chance to study abroad! Through this program, BELTEI students can:

✈️ Study in Another Country:

  • Spend one semester at a partner university overseas.
  • Take specific courses prepared by the foreign university.
  • Experience a different educational environment.

🌎 Live and Learn Abroad:

  • Experience new cultures firsthand.
  • Meet international students, share experiences, and build global connections.
  • Learn new languages and adapt to different lifestyles.

🛠 Full Support from Norton University:

  • Help with visa applications, translations, and embassy interviews.
  • Assistance with passports, travel, and accommodation arrangements.
  • Direct contact with the partner university to ensure a smooth exchange experience.
