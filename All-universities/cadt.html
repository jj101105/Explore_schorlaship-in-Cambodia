Cambodian Academy of Digital Technology (CADT)

CADT is a leading institution in Cambodia, offering cutting-edge programs in digital technology and ICT fields. It focuses on training students with the skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry, fostering innovation and excellence in the digital world.

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art Computer Labs
  • Modern Study Areas
  • Advanced Research Centers
  • Interactive Learning Spaces

Vision

The vision of CADT is to be a leader in the development of digital technology professionals in Cambodia, providing high-quality education and training to meet the growing demand in the digital industry.

Mission

The mission of CADT is to provide world-class education in digital technologies, offering a range of programs that equip students with the practical skills and knowledge needed for successful careers in the global digital economy.

Student Exchange Program

    CADT’s Student Exchange Program promotes diversity, cross-cultural exchange, and global learning experiences. One of the key initiatives is the “Passage to ASEAN (P2A)” Southeast Asian Studies program, which gives students an in-depth understanding of ASEAN history, economics, and culture through partnerships with institutions like Rangsit University. This program helps students develop essential skills to navigate Southeast Asia’s rapidly evolving digital landscape while promoting international collaboration and mutual understanding.
