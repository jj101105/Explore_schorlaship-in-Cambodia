Khemarak University (KU)

Founded in 2005, Khemarak University provides a high standard of education with a focus on developing professionals who are ready to meet the demands of the ever-evolving global market. KU is committed to providing students with both theoretical and practical knowledge.

Facilities

  • Well-equipped classrooms with modern teaching technologies
  • Computer labs and dedicated study spaces
  • Library with extensive academic resources and online databases
  • Sports and recreational facilities

Vision

To be a leading institution in Cambodia, recognized for providing high-quality education and producing skilled graduates who contribute to society's development.

Mission

  • KU is committed to:
    • Offering affordable and high-quality education.
    • Enhancing academic and professional skills.
    • Promoting community engagement and social responsibility.
    • Building global partnerships for academic excellence.
