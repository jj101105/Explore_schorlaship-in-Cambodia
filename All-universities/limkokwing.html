Limkokwing University of Creative Technology
(LIMKOKWING)

Limkokwing University offers various scholarships and financial aid to support both local and international students. Here are the main types of scholarships available:

  • Limkokwing Scholarships: These scholarships are based on academic excellence, leadership, and extracurricular involvement. They are awarded to both local and international students for various undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
  • International Student Scholarships: Designed for international students who wish to pursue a degree at Limkokwing University, these scholarships help cover tuition fees and provide support for living expenses.
  • Creative Talent Scholarships: Available for students with exceptional creative talent, these scholarships support students who excel in fields such as art, design, multimedia, and technology.

Limkokwing University of Creative Technology

Limkokwing University of Creative Technology, established in 1991, is a private institution that emphasizes creativity, innovation, and technology. The university has campuses in Malaysia, Botswana, Cambodia, and several other countries. It offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs with a strong focus on design, multimedia, business, and communication.

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art Creative Studios
  • Fully Equipped Classrooms
  • Library with Digital Resources
  • Computer Labs
  • Design and Multimedia Labs
  • Cafeteria and Recreation Areas

Vision

To be the leading global university in creative education and innovation, producing world-class graduates in various fields of creativity and technology.

Mission

Limkokwing University aims to provide students with a unique education that merges creativity, technology, and business, preparing them for success in a rapidly changing global market.

Sala is currently offering a 30% scholarship coupon code to all students, with no test or exam required. Students who receive a coupon code (voucher) in physical form from Sala can directly go to Limkokwing University’s admission office to register with the coupon.

Financial Assistance

The university offers financial assistance to students in genuine financial difficulty. Students in need of support can write to the Student Service Department and consult with their education counselor. Special assistance will be given to deserving students based on the following criteria:

  • Must be able to prove that they are undergoing financial difficulty.
  • Must have good academic performance.
  • Must be reasonably active in extracurricular activities.
  • Must have the support and recommendation of their school.

Exchange Program / Transfer to Other Campuses

Students wishing to pursue their studies at Limkokwing campuses abroad can consult with their education counselor about the transfer process. Students should apply at least 2 months before the start of the semester to allow time for visa processing and other necessary documentation.
