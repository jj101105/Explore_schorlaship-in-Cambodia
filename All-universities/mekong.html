Cambodian Mekong University Exchange Programs

Cambodian Mekong University offers various exchange programs to promote international academic collaboration, cultural exchange, and global exposure for students. Here are some key programs:

  • International College (CMU-IC): CMU-IC offers English-based undergraduate programs in International Business and Global Entrepreneurship & Innovation. Courses are taught in English by both international and local professors emphasizing experiential learning and action-oriented instruction. [Source]
  • International Partnerships and Scholarships: Cambodian Mekong University has established partnerships with universities in Europe and Asia, facilitating exchange programs that allow students to study abroad and enrich their learning experiences. [Source]
  • SHARE Scholarship Programme: Cambodian Mekong University participates in the SHARE Scholarship Programme, offering fully-funded, one-semester exchange opportunities for undergraduate students from selected ASEAN universities. This program promotes regional integration and cultural exchange. [Source]
  • Exchange Opportunities: Cambodian Mekong University offers exchange programs with various universities in Europe and Asia, allowing students to gain international academic experience and expand their global network. [Source]
  • Scholarships for International Exchanges: The university actively participates in national and regional entrepreneurship competitions, offering scholarships and financial support to students engaged in international exchanges. [Source]
