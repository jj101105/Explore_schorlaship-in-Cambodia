RULE offers various scholarships and financial aid to support both local and international students. Here are the main types of scholarships available:

  • Dual Degree Scholarships: Offered through the French Cooperation Department, these scholarships allow students to earn European-accredited dual degrees in law, economics, and management in partnership with Paris University, University of Brussels – ULB, and University of Montreal.

Royal University of Law and Economics

The Royal University of Law and Economics (RULE) is Cambodia's oldest higher education institution, established in 1949 as the National Institute of Law, Politics, and Economics. In 1957, it was renamed the Faculty of Law and Economic Sciences and integrated into the University of Phnom Penh. After a period of closure during the Khmer Rouge regime, it reopened in 1982 as the Administrative and Judicial School, later becoming the Faculty of Law in 1992. Scholarships

Since 1994, RULE has provided scholarship opportunities for students to pursue Master’s and PhD programs at various prestigious universities worldwide, including Australia, Canada, France, Japan, Korea, New Zealand, Spain, Switzerland, Sweden, Thailand, Singapore, the United States, and Vietnam.

Available Scholarships

  • Nagoya University Scholarship: Two annual scholarships for Law students to study in Japan.
  • TLBU Scholarship: A scholarship to study at Transnational Law and Business University (TLBU) in Korea.
  • French Cooperation Scholarships: Around 10 students per year receive scholarships, primarily awarded by the Eiffel and BGF programs, to pursue Master's and PhD degrees in France.
  • International Mobility Scholarships: In collaboration with foreign universities, RULE offers scholarships for exchange programs, such as the University of Bologna's mobility scholarship.
  • External Scholarships: Various international scholarships are available through platforms like WeMakeScholars, providing financial aid for different degrees.

For detailed eligibility and application procedures, please check RULE’s official website or contact their admissions office.

🌍 RULE Student Exchange Program

RULE strongly supports student exchange opportunities, allowing students to gain international exposure through short-term and long-term exchanges. As a member of the AUN Network and European Union Projects, RULE students have participated in exchanges across ASEAN and beyond, including countries such as Singapore, Indonesia, China, France, Germany, Spain, Turkey, Belgium, Canada, Japan, India, and Estonia.

✈️ Eligibility and Requirements:

  • Students must be officially nominated by RULE.
  • Tuition fees continue to be paid to RULE while studying abroad.
  • Exchange opportunities range from one semester (six months) to one year.
