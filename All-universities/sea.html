Southeast Asia University (SEA)

Established in 1997, SEA is recognized by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sport of Cambodia. The university emphasizes practical learning, modern education methods, and the development of professional skills for the 21st century.

Facilities

  • Multimedia-equipped classrooms
  • Computer and language labs
  • Well-stocked library and e-resources
  • Clubs, competitions, and community engagement programs

Vision

To become a leading private university providing high-quality education and producing competent graduates in Cambodia.

Mission

  • SEA is committed to:
    • Providing accessible and affordable higher education.
    • Enhancing teaching, learning, and research.
    • Promoting innovation and practical skills.
    • Building networks for local and international collaboration.

    • Academics
