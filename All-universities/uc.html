The University of Cambodia
(UC)

UC offers various scholarships and financial aid to support both local students. Here are the main types of scholarships available:

Key Scholarships

  • Samdech Techo Hun Sen Vision-100 Scholarships: Main program, funded by donors and organizations.
  • Private Scholarships: Given by individuals, no exam required.
  • Additional Scholarships (2016): Includes Best Achievers, Sports, Research Assistant, and more.
  • UC Peace Scholarships (2021-2025): 500 awards through a competitive exam.

These scholarships help students with tuition and academic support.

Other Scholarship Programs

  • Best Achievers Scholarships: Awarded to top-performing students.
  • Excellence Scholarships: Given to students with outstanding academic records.
  • Outstanding Student Scholarships: For students demonstrating exceptional achievements.
  • High School Top Student Scholarships: Recognizing top high school graduates.
  • Sports Scholarships: For students excelling in athletics.
  • ASEAN Young Leaders Scholarships: Supporting future leaders from ASEAN countries.
  • TSS Scholarships: Aimed at talented students in specific fields.
  • Research Assistant Scholarships: Supporting students involved in research.
  • Teaching Assistant Scholarships: For students assisting in academic teaching.
  • Graduate Teaching Assistant Scholarships: For graduate students taking on teaching roles.
  • Partial Scholarships: Providing partial tuition assistance.
  • Center for English Studies Partial Scholarships: Helping students improve English proficiency.

The University of Cambodia (UC) is one of Cambodia‚Äôs leading higher education institutions, established in 2003. It offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across multiple disciplines, emphasizing quality education, research, and innovation.

Facilities

Vision

To be a leading university in Cambodia and internationally recognized for academic excellence, research, and innovation.

Mission

UC is committed to providing high-quality education, fostering research and innovation, promoting global cooperation, and preparing students for leadership roles in their respective fields.
