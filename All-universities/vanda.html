VANDA offers various scholarships and financial aid to support both local students. Here are the main types of scholarships available:

  • 1. Government Scholarships: scholarships are provided to students who actively participate in the community or come from disadvantaged families in specific regions or provinces that are prioritized.
  • 2. Academic Scholarships : These scholarships are granted based on the individual academic achievements of students, and the benefits depend on the student's performance.
  • 3. Community Service Scholarships: These scholarships are awarded to students who have been actively involved in community service and have demonstrated positive influence within their communities.

VANNDA Institute

Established in 1997 as Vanda Accounting Center, it was renamed Vanda Institute in 2001. While the center still operates under the institute’s management, "institute" was chosen for its broader scope. Vanda Institute now has three branches in Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, and Battambang.
