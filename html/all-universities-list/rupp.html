The International Relations Office of the Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) is honored to announce the Batch 3 of the SHARE Scholarship for exchange programs. This allows students to spend one academic semester or up to six months in ASEAN universities.

For RUPP Students

Students who wish to join SHARE participating universities must follow these steps:

  1. Download the required forms (fillable on a computer).
  2. Research relevant subjects on host university websites.
  3. Complete the application for endorsement from RUPP.
  4. Apply for a Letter of Endorsement from the respective department and RUPP-IRO.
  5. Submit an application to the Host University for the exchange program.
  6. Once accepted, apply for the SHARE Scholarship.
  7. Commit to participating in a two-week Pre-Departure Course.
  8. More details: www.share-asean.eu/scholarship

Note: This scholarship is primarily for students in Years 2 and 3. Year 4 students must consult their respective department.

For International Students

The International students who wish to have exchange programs with RUPP under Share scheme (second batch) should follow the steps below:

  1. Submit an endorsement letter from your home university.
  2. Complete the application for acceptance at RUPP.
  3. Provide a scanned copy of your official transcript.
  4. Submit proof of English language proficiency.
  5. Contact RUPP-IRO or the respective department of interest.
  6. Download the Application for Acceptance from the RUPP admissions portal.

The Exchange Program is under the SHARE Scheme. Based on the SHARE Quota, we accept:

  • 6 International students to DIS
  • 2 International students to the English Department
  • 2 International students to DMC
