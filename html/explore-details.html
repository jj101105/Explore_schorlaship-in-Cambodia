Overseas Study Scholarship

Supporting Cambodian students in pursuing education abroad.

About the Scholarship

The **Overseas Study Scholarship** helps Cambodian students study in top international universities by providing tuition assistance, travel support, and living stipends.

🎓 Scholarship Benefits:

  • 🏫 Covers **partial or full tuition fees**.
  • ✈️ Provides **travel and visa support**.
  • 💰 Offers **monthly stipends for accommodation & living expenses**.
  • 📚 Supports students pursuing **bachelor’s & master’s degrees abroad**.

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✅ Must be a **Cambodian citizen**.
  • ✅ Must have **strong academic performance**.
  • ✅ Must have received an offer from an **accredited international university**.
  • ✅ Demonstrate **financial need or academic excellence**.

Required Documents

  • 📄 High school/university transcripts
  • 📜 Admission letter from the overseas university
  • 📝 Personal statement explaining your study goals
  • 👨‍🏫 Recommendation letters
  • 🆔 Copy of passport & visa documents

How to Apply

  • 🌍 Step 1: Research and apply to international universities.
  • 📂 Step 2: Gather required documents.
  • ✉️ Step 3: Submit scholarship application via the official website.
  • 📞 Step 4: Attend an interview (if required).
  • ✅ Step 5: Receive the scholarship decision and prepare for your studies!

📌 Tips for Studying Abroad

  • Prepare early 📌 Visa applications & admission take time.
  • Improve your language skills 📌 Some universities require English proficiency tests like IELTS or TOEFL.
  • Budget wisely 📌 Plan your living expenses and explore part-time work options.
  • Connect with alumni 📌 Join online groups of Cambodian students studying abroad.
