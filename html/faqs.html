Frequently Asked Questions

Why was Scholarships in Cambodia created?

➕

We created this platform to help students in Cambodia easily find scholarships, get guidance, and receive financial aid for their education.

How do we support students?

➕

We provide free access to a large database of scholarships, offer mentorship programs, and share application tips to increase your chances of success.

Who can apply for these scholarships?

➕

Our platform provides opportunities for high school graduates, university students, and postgraduate candidates. Each scholarship has its own criteria.

How do I apply for a scholarship?

➕

Visit our scholarship page, choose an opportunity that fits your eligibility, and follow the application instructions carefully.

What documents do I need to apply?

➕

Most scholarships require transcripts, recommendation letters, a personal statement, and proof of financial need.

Are there scholarships for international studies?

➕

Yes, we provide information on scholarships for Cambodian students who want to study abroad in different countries.

Can I apply for multiple scholarships?

➕

Yes, you can apply for multiple scholarships, as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each one.

What should I do if my application is rejected?

➕

If your application is rejected, don’t be discouraged. Learn from feedback, improve your documents, and reapply next year.

Do scholarships cover full tuition fees?

➕

Some scholarships cover full tuition, while others are partial. Always check the scholarship details to understand what’s covered.

Where can I get help with my application?

➕

You can seek guidance from school counselors, university advisors, or online forums that specialize in scholarships.
