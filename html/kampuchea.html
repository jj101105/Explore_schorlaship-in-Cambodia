Higher Education Grant of Cambodia

Financial aid for students in rural Cambodia to access higher education opportunities.

About the Scholarship

The ** Higher Education Grant of Cambodia** is designed to support students from **rural areas** in Cambodia by providing financial aid for tuition fees, learning materials, and living expenses.

📌 Scholarship Benefits:

  • 🏫 Covers **50-100% of tuition fees** based on financial need.
  • 📚 Provides **funding for textbooks & study materials**.
  • 🏠 Offers **monthly stipends for housing & living expenses**.
  • 🎓 Supports students pursuing **undergraduate degrees in Cambodia**.

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✅ Must be a **Cambodian citizen** from a rural area.
  • ✅ Must have completed **high school with good academic performance**.
  • ✅ Family income must be **below the financial aid threshold**.
  • ✅ Must be **admitted to an accredited university in Cambodia**.

Required Documents

  • 📄 High school diploma & transcripts
  • 📑 Proof of family income
  • 📝 Personal statement (why you need this scholarship)
  • 👨‍🏫 Recommendation letter from a teacher or community leader
  • 🆔 Copy of national ID or passport
