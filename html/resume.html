Resume Structure Guide

Follow this recommended format to structure your resume clearly and professionally.

1. Personal Info

Full name, phone, email, LinkedIn/portfolio (optional)

2. Education

Your university, major, GPA, graduation year

3. Skills

Highlight technical and soft skills relevant to the program/scholarship

4. Experience

Internships, projects, volunteer work, achievements

5. Awards & Certificates

Any awards, online courses, or certificates
